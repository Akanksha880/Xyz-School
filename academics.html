Academics at Senior High Secondary School Jammu

A Curriculum Designed for Excellence and Innovation

Primary Education (Grades 1-5)

Our primary education program focuses on foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.

Subjects include English, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and regional languages, with an emphasis on interactive learning.

Students engage in project-based learning, arts, and physical education to foster creativity and teamwork.

Secondary Education (Grades 6-12)

Our secondary curriculum prepares students for higher education with advanced courses in science, mathematics, humanities, and languages.

We offer streams in Science, Commerce, and Arts, tailored to individual career aspirations.

Regular assessments and counseling ensure students are on track for competitive exams and university admissions.

Extracurricular Programs

Sports

Programs in cricket, football, basketball, and more to promote physical fitness.

Arts

Painting, music, and drama to nurture creativity and self-expression.

Clubs

Debate, robotics, and environmental clubs to develop leadership skills.

Special Programs

STEM Program

Hands-on learning in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

Language Immersion

Advanced courses in French, German, and regional languages.

Leadership Training

Workshops to develop communication and leadership skills.

Career Counseling

Guidance for university admissions and career planning.
