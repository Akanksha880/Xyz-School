About Senior High Secondary School Jammu

A Legacy of Excellence in Education Since 1985

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

We are committed to providing a nurturing environment where students develop critical thinking, creativity, and leadership skills to excel in a global society.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in education, fostering innovation, inclusivity, and holistic development for every student.

Our values include integrity, excellence, and community, ensuring every student feels valued and empowered.

Our History

1985 - Foundation

Established in Jammu with a vision to provide quality education to the region.

1995 - Campus Expansion

Expanded our campus with modern facilities, including science labs and a library.

2005 - International Recognition

Received national awards for excellence in education and community service.

2020 - Digital Integration

Embraced technology with virtual classrooms and e-learning platforms.
